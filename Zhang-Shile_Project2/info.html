Overview

This website is suitable for people who study design courses or working as a designer. Designers have limited time so that they needa method to help them to develop they ideas or create a idea for them. Bingo!Idea is a idea generator, which will be very useful when they run out the ideas or need more inspiration.

Creating

Users can randomly enter keywords to search images based on the keywords. They can choose a suitable image for their ideas and the system can analyize some key elements from the image chosen by the user. Users can merge the elements to a maximum 5 elements for they idea. Based on those 5 elements (or less), the system will generate image from the elements. Alternatively, EEG equipment helps them generate the best image based on their brain waves.
